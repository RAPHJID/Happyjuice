Frequently Asked Questions

These are by far the most frequently asked questions. But if you have more questions, feel free to contact us for more details.

Do you offer private parties?
Yes, we do! We cater private parties of all sizes — birthdays, weddings, or corporate events. Our bartenders mix up refreshing cocktails and mocktails on the spot, ensuring every guest gets a drink they’ll love.
Do you provide custom menus for your events?
Absolutely. We design custom cocktail menus to match your event’s theme or vibe. Whether you want classics like Mojitos and Margaritas, or unique signature cocktails crafted just for your occasion, we’ll make it happen.
What are the rates for your service?
Our rates depend on the type of event, the number of guests, and the cocktail menu you’d like. We offer flexible packages that cover bar setup, mixers, and professional bartenders. Contact us to get a tailored quote for your event.
